According to the CDC, 4,400 people die to bullying each year.

Leading it to be 3rd leading cause of death amongst young people.

Bullying victims are between 2 to 9 times more likely to consider suicide than non-victims.

A study in Britain found that at least half of suicides among young people are related to bullying.

According to statistics reported by ABC News, nearly 30 percent of students are either bullies or victims of bullying, and 160,000 kids stay home from school every day because of fear of bullying.
